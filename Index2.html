How to play

Three positions: Fly, Neutral, Crouch. An obstacle has exactly one deadly position. If you're in that position when it reaches you, you die. The other two positions are safe.

Controls:

  • Desktop AZERTY: Z=Fly, S=Crouch, Q=Left, D=Right. Arrow keys and WASD are supported too.
  • Mobile Mode: press Mobile Mode (or the Mobile icon) to go canvas-fullscreen and show top-right directional pad (Up/Down/Left/Right).

Levels are longer now and obstacles vary more so you can't simply stay in one position and win.
